Network Architecture

  • D. D. Clark, The Design Philosophy of the DARPA Internet Protocols, SIGCOMM 1988

  • J. H. Saltzer, D. P. Reed, D. D. Clark, End-to-End Arguments in System Design, Distributed Computing Systems, 1981.

  • M. Handley, Why the Internet only just works, BT Technology Journal, 24(3), July 2006.

  • D. D. Clark. Tussle in Cyberspace: Defining Tomorrow’s Internet. ToN 2005.

Resource Management

End-to-end congestion control

  • V. Jacobson and M. Karels, Congestion Avoidance and Control, expanded version of ACM SIGCOMM 1988 paper.

Network assisted congestion control

  • D. Katabi, M. Handley, C. Rohrs, Congestion control for high bandwidth-delay product networks, SIGCOMM 2002.

  • N. Dukkipati, N. McKeown, Why Flow-Completion Time is the Right Metric for Congestion Control, SIGCOMM CCR 2006.

Active queue management

  • S. Floyd and V. Jacobson, Random Early Detection Gateways for Congestion Avoidance, IEEE/ACM Trans. on Networking, 1(4), Aug. 1993.

  • C. Hollot et al. Analysis and design of controllers for AQM routers supporting TCP flows. IEEE Transactions on automatic control 47.6 (2002): 945-959. [MA: Probably too theoretical.]

  • PIE RFC

Scheduling and Fairness

  • A. Demers et al. Analysis and simulation of a fair queueing algorithm, SIGCOMM 1989.

  • A. Parekh and R. Gallager, A generalized processor sharing approach to flow control in integrated services networks: the single-node case, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking (TON) 1993.

  • I. Stoica et al. Core-Stateless Fair Queueing: Achieving Approximately Fair Bandwidth Allocations in High Speed Networks, SIGCOMN 1998.

  • B. Briscoe, Flow Rate Fairness: Dismantling a Religion, SIGCOMM CCR 2007.

Modern congestion control

  • D. Wischik et al., Design, implementation and evaluation of congestion control for multipath TCP, NSDI 2011.

  • K. Winstein et al., Stochastic Forecasts Achieve High Throughput and Low Delay over Cellular Networks, NSDI 2013.

  • K. Winstein et al., TCP ex Machina: Computer-Generated Congestion Control, SIGCOMM 2013.

  • M. Dong et al., PCC: Re-architecting Congestion Control for Consistent High Performance, NSDI 2015. [MA: In case you want to spend a lecture slamming the paper!]

Router design

Classic

  • T. Anderson et al., High-speed Switch Scheduling for Local-area Networks, ACM Trans. Computer Systems, 1993.

  • N. McKeown, The iSLIP Scheduling Algorithm for Input-Queued Switches, IEEE/ACM Trans. on Networking, 9(2), April 1999.

Buffer sizing

  • G. Appenzeller et al., Sizing Router Buffers, SIGCOMM 2004.

Software routers

  • E. Kohler et al., The Click Modular Router, ACM TOCS 2000.

  • M. Dobrescu et al., RouteBricks: Exploiting Parallelism To Scale Software Routers, SOSP 2009.

Programmable dataplanes

  • P. Bosshart et al., P4: Programming Protocol-Independent Packet Processors, SIGCOMM CCR 2014.

  • P. Bosshart et al., Forwarding Metamorphosis: Fast Programmable Match-Action Processing in Hardware for SDN, SIGCOMM 2013.
